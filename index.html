Questions & Answers

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before booking with us

What areas in Lagos do you serve?

We serve all major areas in Lagos including Ikeja, Victoria Island, Lekki, Ikoyi, Surulere, Yaba, and surrounding neighborhoods. Contact us to confirm your specific location.

Do you provide cleaning supplies and equipment?

Yes! We bring all necessary supplies, equipment, and eco-friendly products. If you prefer we use your specific products, we're happy to accommodate that.

How do you determine pricing?

Pricing is based on the size of the space, type of cleaning, and frequency of service. We provide transparent quotes with zero hidden fees after assessing your needs.

Are your cleaners insured and background checked?

Absolutely. All our cleaners undergo thorough background checks, are fully insured, and receive comprehensive training to ensure the highest quality service.

What is your cancellation policy?

You can cancel or reschedule with at least 24 hours notice at no charge. Last-minute cancellations may incur a small fee.
